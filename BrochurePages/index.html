Task & Member

Quirky self-managed club system

Managing a voluntary group with tasks to complete


A database driven website, under your control, to manage a group of members with a simplified tracking of tasks to complete or lessons to study.

Progress can be checked as each student completes stages of the task or lesson.

Members can self-select to tackle tasks or lessons or can be assigned by managers.

New tasks or lessons can be created with steps or stages.


Each webpage has a menu along the top of the available actions on that page plus a manage link to the other pages

There are two main administration webpages. One for information about people & the other for information about tasks.

Carrying out tasks is split into separate pages.

People.html


Summary of members, students managers or authors.


How to use: Clicking any menu item on the page will display a page of people from the database and display them on a table that opens automatically.

Clicking any row of the table will open a card display of that row of information

In the card display there are navigation buttons previous and next to change which item is displayed, but the new item is only displayed after you click the displayed number to confirm the change. (The highest number is always limited to prevenmt trying to read a row that does not exist.)

Tasks.html


Summary of task headers, the stages of each task &the assigned tasks on the tasklist.


How to use: Clicking any menu item on the tasks page will display a page of tasks from the database and display them on a table that opens automatically.

Clicking any row of the table will open a card display of that row of information

In the card display there are navigation buttons previous and next to change which item is displayed, but the new item is only displayed after you click the displayed number to confirm the change. (The highest number is always limited to prevenmt trying to read a row that does not exist.)
